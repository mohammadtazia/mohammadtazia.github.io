I want to create a system that not only produces consistent, effective action, but also one that will do so in perpetuity.

Precedents

Staggered initiative planning will be implemented: initiatives will be planned during the first half of each month and projected to occur during the first half of the next, allowing time for brainstorming during the second half of every month. Different members of the activism department will be organizing these initiatives during alternating months, allowing initiatives to occur on a continual basis.

A monthly calendar will be released detailing deadlines for the advocacy department.
